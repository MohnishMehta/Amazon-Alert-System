How to use amazon alert system

Step 1: Head over to the alert system page!

Step 2: Fill out your name in the input-field for name!

Step 3: Head over to amazon and grab the link of the item you want to keep on lookout!

Step 4: Paste that link in the input-field for amazon product link!

Step 5: Type in the price you would be willing to buy at in the desired price input-field!

Step 6: Type in your gmail in the input-field for email!

Step 7: Type in your app password (this is not your email password. Hit the app password tab to see how to get your app password) in the input-field app password

Step 8: Hit submit and wait for an email!
